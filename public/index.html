⬡Thinking
1 Strategy Phase
  • Speaker picks first, then clockwise
  • Each player takes 1 of 8 strategy cards
  • Lower card number = earlier initiative this round
  • Primary: yours alone · Secondary: others spend 1 STRAT CC
  • Unpicked cards each gain 3 Trade Goods at Status Phase
2 Action Phase
  • Players alternate in initiative order (lowest card first)
  • Tactical: place CC, move ships, produce units
  • Strategic: use your card's primary; others use secondary
  • Component: play action card, tech, or [ACTION] ability
  • Pass: once done, you're out; phase ends when all pass
3 Status Phase
  • Score up to 1 public + 1 secret objective — don't forget!
  • Reveal next public objective card
  • Repair all Sustained Damage units
  • Return all CCs from board → pools; ready all planets
  • Draw action cards · Pass Speaker token clockwise
4 Agenda Phase (Round 3+)
  • Reveal 2 agenda cards; vote on each separately
  • Exhaust planets to spend their Influence as votes
  • Laws = permanent · Directives = one-time effect
  • Speaker votes last and breaks all ties
  • Vote FOR or AGAINST each outcome — abstaining loses influence
Command Token Pools
Tactics Pool
  • Spent to activate systems (move ships + produce)
  • Most common spend — manage carefully
Fleet Pool
  • Caps non-fighter ships per system you control
  • Exceed limit → must remove ships immediately
Strategy Pool
  • Spent to use secondary abilities on others' strategy cards
  • Start with 2; rarely replenished mid-round
Space Combat Sequence
1
Anti-Fighter Barrage — destroyers and some flagships fire at enemy fighters only. Target cannot return fire. (If no destroyers vs fighters, skip)
2
Space Cannon Offense — PDS units in adjacent systems fire at ships. Happens before combat begins. (If no PDS in range, skip)
3
Announce Retreat — attacker can declare intent to retreat to an adjacent system they control. (Retreat executes at end of round)
4
Combat Round (repeat until someone retreats or is destroyed):
a) Both players roll dice — each ship rolls once, hits on its combat value or higher
b) Assign hits — choose which ships take hits; Sustain Damage absorbs 1 hit without dying
c) Retreat resolves if announced; otherwise continue
Ground Combat (if attacker lands troops)
1
Bombardment — attacking dreadnoughts and war suns roll dice to destroy defending infantry and PDS. (Before troops land)
2
Space Cannon Defense — defender's PDS fire at landing troops. (If no defending PDS, skip)
3
Ground Combat Rounds — infantry and mechs fight. Attacker wins by eliminating all defenders. Defender wins by having 1 unit survive. Attacker must land at least 1 troop to invade.
Action Card Timing
·
[ACTION] — play on your turn as a Component Action. Costs your entire action for the round.
·
[BEFORE COMBAT] — play after ships move into a system but before any combat steps begin.
·
[COMBAT ROUND START] — play at the start of any combat round, before dice are rolled.
·
[AFTER COMBAT] — play after the final combat round resolves, before any post-combat steps.
